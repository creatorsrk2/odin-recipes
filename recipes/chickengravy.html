Boneless Chicken Gravy

Description

This is a tasty recipe.

It is going to be thick.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken
  • 2 chopped onions
  • 2 chopped tomatoes
  • 5 slit green chillies
  • 2 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  • Marination powders: Chilly powder, Dhaniya Powder, Garam Masala, Cumin powder
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. In a pan, add oil and fry the marinated chicken(with powders and salt) in medium flame
  2. Once it is semi-cooked, keep it aside
  3. In a pan, add oil and fry tomatoes and onions, then add ginger garlic paste and cook until oil oozes out
  4. Put in the chicken, a little bit of water, and cook in medium flame for 20 minutes until the gravy is thick
  5. Serve with rice or rotis
