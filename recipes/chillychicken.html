Andhra Chilly Chicken

Description

This is a fantastic recipe.

It is going to be spicy. So, eat at your own risk :)

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken
  • 50 grams green chillies
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp corn flour
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Grind the chillies to make a coarse paste and add it to the chicken
  2. Add salt, soy sauce and ginger garlic paste to the chicken. Mix together
  3. Let it cook for 3 to 4 whistles
  4. Once the cooker cools down, cook in medium heat until majority of the water evaporates
  5. Add cornflour-mixed-water to it, mix well and cook in high flame for 2 minutes
  6. Serve with rice or rotis
