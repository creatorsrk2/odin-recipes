Paneer Masala

Description

This is a delicious recipe.

Try not to over eat :)

Ingredients

  • 250 grams paneer cubes
  • 2 chopped onions
  • 2 chopped tomatoes
  • Coconut milk, Cooking cream, coriander leaves
  • 2 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  • Powders: Chilly powder, Dhaniya Powder, Cumin powder
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. In a pan, add oil and fry the onions until medium brown
  2. Add tomatoes and powders along with ginger garlic paste until oil starts oozing out of the gravy
  3. Add some water, coconut milk and cook for a few minutes
  4. Add cooking cream, give it a good mix and add paneer cubes
  5. Cook the paneer in medium heat for 5 mins. Add coriander powder and give it a mix
  6. Serve with rice or rotis
